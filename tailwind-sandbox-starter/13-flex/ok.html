Flex Demo

Same three items in each row, only changing the flex value of the middle one.

Middle item: flex: none; (flex-none)
Item 1 (width: 200px)
Item 2
flex: none;
(no grow, no shrink)
Item 3 (width: 200px)

flex-none: Middle item keeps its set width and will not grow or shrink. If the container is too small, it just overflows.

Middle item: flex: 0 1 auto; (flex-initial)
Item 1 (width: 200px)
Item 2
flex: 0 1 auto;
(can shrink, no grow)
Item 3 (width: 200px)

flex-initial: Middle item starts from its natural width, can shrink if needed, but will not grow to take extra space.

Middle item: flex: 1 1 auto; (flex-auto)
Item 1 (width: 200px)
Item 2
flex: 1 1 auto;
(grow & shrink,
starting from content width)
Item 3 (width: 200px)

flex-auto: Middle item can grow and shrink, but its natural size (content / width) still matters when space is shared.

Middle item: flex: 1 1 0%; (flex-1)
Item 1 (width: 200px)
Item 2
flex: 1 1 0%;
(grow & shrink,
start from 0)
Item 3 (width: 200px)

flex-1: Middle item grows and shrinks, but its base size is treated as 0. It tries to share the available space evenly with other flex items that can grow.
